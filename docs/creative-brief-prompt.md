# Claude design prompt — "Keep more of what you earn" creative

Paste the block below into Claude (in design/artifact mode). It generates a
single high-converting marketing creative built around Seshn's flat-10% value
prop and the fee-comparison story. All figures are sourced in
`docs/marketing-sources.md`.

---

You are a senior art director and conversion copywriter. Design a single,
self-contained, responsive marketing creative (one HTML artifact, inline CSS,
no external assets or fonts beyond system/Google fonts) for **Seshn** — a
collaboration marketplace for working musicians. The creative's one job is to
make an independent musician feel "I keep more of my money here" and click
**Start your session**.

**Brand & tone**
- Personality: a recording studio, not a SaaS dashboard. Confident, warm,
  pro-musician, anti-gatekeeper. Plain-spoken, never corporate.
- Visual language: studio/console motifs — channel strips, faders, VU meters,
  waveform, "REC" tally light, gain-staging. Dark "control room" aesthetic with
  one accent color (a vivid green/lime, like a signal LED) on near-black, plus a
  warm off-white for text.
- Type: a strong geometric/display face for headlines (e.g. Space Grotesk /
  Clash-style), clean sans for body. Tight, confident letter-spacing.
- Tagline to echo: **"Make a record. Together."** / "Find the people who finish
  your record."

**The core message (this is the hook)**
"Build a music career without a whole team taking a cut." On every **$1,000**
earned from a paid collaboration:
- Major-label deal → you keep ≈ **$150–200** (label keeps ~80%, recoups first)
- Manager only (15–20%) → ≈ **$800–850**
- The full team stack (manager 20% + agent 10% + business mgr 5% + legal 5% ≈ 40%) → ≈ **$600**
- **Seshn — flat 10%, Stripe fees included → you keep $900**

Make the **$900 / Seshn** row the unmistakable hero (accent fill, a glowing
fader pushed highest, a checkmark). The others are muted comparison bars.

**Required elements**
1. A magnetic headline (≤7 words) + one-line subhead.
2. The comparison as a visual — render the four options as **console faders or
   horizontal "you keep" bars**, where bar length = money kept. Seshn's is the
   tallest/longest and lit up.
3. A one-line trust strip: "One flat 10%. Stripe processing included. No manager,
   label, or agent required to get paid. Split & trade collabs are free."
4. A single primary CTA button: **▶ Start your session**.
5. A small, credible footnote: "Typical published industry rates — manager
   15–20%, agent ~10%, label royalty 15–20% before recoupment. Sources on
   request."

**Conversion craft**
- One idea, one CTA, zero clutter. The eye should go headline → Seshn bar → CTA.
- Use contrast and size to create a single focal point (the $900).
- Numbers are the proof; don't bury them in prose.
- Mobile-first: it must look great at 1080×1350 (portrait social) AND reflow to a
  wide 1200×628 banner. Provide a toggle or note both framings.

**Deliverable**
- One polished HTML artifact I can screenshot for paid social (Instagram/TikTok
  feed + a landscape banner variant).
- Then give me **3 alternative headline options** and **2 alternative accent
  color directions** below the artifact.

Do not invent statistics beyond the ones provided. Keep it honest, sharp, and
unmistakably about musicians keeping their money.
